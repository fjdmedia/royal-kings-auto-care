05 — Booking Form

Same form, sharper CTA

The booking form was already solid. The upgrade tightens the CTA copy and adds some guardrails that catch issues before they become problems (24-hour minimum, 90-day max, spam protection).

Current — "Request Appointment"
Upgraded — "Book My Detail"

First-person CTA converts higher

"Book My Detail" reads like the customer's own decision. Tested to convert better than passive copy like "Request Appointment."

Date guardrails

Minimum 24 hours in advance. Maximum 90 days out. Stops bookings you can't fulfill and keeps the calendar clean.

Silent spam protection

Invisible honeypot field blocks bot submissions. Real humans don't see it — spammers fill it and get rejected.

07 — NEW  Mobile Experience

Sticky "Book Now" bar for phone users

Most of your traffic comes from phones — Instagram taps, Google searches on mobile, TikTok bio clicks. The sticky "Book Now" bar stays pinned to the bottom of the screen the entire time they're scrolling, so booking is always one tap away.

  • Always one tap from booking No scrolling up to find the form. Pinned to the bottom of the screen while they scroll.
  • Gold bar = brand-aligned Matches the site palette. Professional, not aggressive. Familiar pattern users already know.
  • Safe-area aware (iPhone notch) Respects the notch and bottom home indicator on newer iPhones — doesn't get cut off.
  • Only appears on mobile Desktop users don't see it — it would just take up screen real estate they don't need.
08 — Under The Hood

The invisible upgrades — the ones Google cares about

These are changes you won't see at a glance, but they're the ones that separate a hobbyist site from a professional one. They affect search ranking, load speed, and who can actually use the site.

SEO

LocalBusiness schema for Google

Tells Google you're an auto-detailing shop in Winnipeg (not just some website). Unlocks "near me" search results.

SEO

Open Graph + Twitter Cards

When someone shares your link in a text or on Instagram, it now shows a proper preview card with your logo — not a broken thumbnail.

Speed

Deferred script loading

Content paints first, scripts load after. Page feels faster even on slow mobile connections.

Speed

Explicit image dimensions

Page doesn't "jump" as images load. Smoother scroll, better scores on Google's Core Web Vitals.

Accessibility

Keyboard-friendly FAQ

Tab + Enter opens accordion items. Screen readers announce the right labels. Meets WCAG AA contrast standards.

Accessibility

Safe-area padding on sticky CTA

iPhones with notches don't cut off the "Book Now" button anymore — displays correctly on every phone model.

Security

Honeypot spam protection

Invisible form field that bots fill in and humans don't. Blocks garbage submissions from ever hitting your inbox.

Trust

Digital waiver + Drive archive

Signed waivers are saved straight to your Google Drive. Deposit policy displays on both form and waiver — no disputes.
